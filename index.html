x BOARD

xBoard is an app designed to help expand a user’s keyboard by tying the screen of the user’s phone to functions and macros of a regular computer. The screen’s layout and functions can then be customized to the individual user’s needs. These configurations can be saved and reloaded.

This site hosts the computer client software that connects to your phone.
